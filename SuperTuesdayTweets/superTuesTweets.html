Super Tuesday Tweets Visualized

View the code on Github

Super Tuesday Tweets Visualized is an interactive dashboard of tweets that I collected on Super Tuesday for the 2012 election. It was built using the d3.js library as a part of my independent study on visualizing government data. States that were holding primaries that day are highlighed in red on the map.

To get started, either enter in a term in the search bar below or click on a state from the map, and the number of tweets for each canidate will be displayed. To view some of the tweets involving the canidates on that topic, click on the bar next to the canidates name.

Enter a topic or click on a state to see the total number of tweets that relate to the search terms below.
